ABOUT ME

My Story

Although I pursued a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology in college, I have always been drawn back to web design and development. At first, I took a basic HTML class at my local community learning center. Then I started tinkering around with templates, assisting friends with getting their websites customized. At the time, I looked at this as a hobby.

As time went on, I found my curiousity for web development had not waned. In fact, it was just as strong as ever and I started to realize that this was the career path I wanted to pursue. I started taking online classes through Codecademy and FreeCodeCamp, but I wasn't satisfied with what I was learning. I wanted to see more of the practical application of the new skills I was learning.

This is when I discovered LearningFuze and their accelerated web development program. It was an intense 12 week program where I put in over 700 hours between instructor lectures and hands on coding. It is here where I first truly understood what it feels like to have passion for I do. The gratification in overcoming challenges, solving problems and creating new applications fuels my desire to keep improving my skills.
